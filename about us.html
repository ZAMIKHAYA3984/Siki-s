Mission

Sikis Koffe Kaffe was established in 2016 as a coffe shop where people from all walks of life could come and drink coffe.

Vision

Sikelela "Siki" Dibela worked for so many years in Cape Town and London in the coffe industry. He had a passion for bringing quality Africaan coffe to the township of Khayelitsha.

The expectation that siki had for his shop was that business would be booming, but the reality of bringing a new commodity was that not many people had experienced a coffe shop in a community so close to home. Siki spent many days watching the locals walk by his shop and wave as they move about their daily agenda.

Eventually more money and economic flow began to happen in Khayelitsha,which in turn led to more creative flows.Siki's quickly become the place to have meeting and discussion on important community issues and has stayed that way up until today.
