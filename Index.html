Siki's Coffee Cafe

No matter what historians claimed,BC really stands for Before Coffee ?

Sikelele Dibela has a passion for bringing quality African Coffee to the township of Khayelitsha.
His shop is in the garage of his mothre's house- avibrant social hub in the heart of the community.
The coffee he serves to his customers is a balanced blend of beans from Ethiopia, Kenya and Burundi-
some of the powerhouse of African coffee culture.The coffee in his bag was carefully selected
by Warren Machanik of Quaffee and expertly blended by his master roaster
Mzukisi Xhati.
